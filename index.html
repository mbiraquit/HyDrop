Company Background

Hydrop was founded in 2023 by 4 Computer Engineering Students (John Paul Galache, Mike Jay-vee Almonte, Melisa Biraquit, Arielle Gemae Javier). HyDrop is a company that offers IoT-based Automated Hydroponic Management System. The product promotes accurary and efficiency in farming. The word HyDrop came from the combination of the words Hydro, greek word for water, and Drop, short for droplets since hydroponics works on water. HyDrop is also a short term for hydroponics itself.
